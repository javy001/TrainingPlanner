Training Planner App

Plan smarter. Train better. This app makes it easy to build your triathlon training plan and stay on track—without the clutter. Whether you’re juggling weekly goals, adjusting workouts to fit your schedule, or just trying to remember how long that ride or swim was supposed to be, this app simplifies it all with a clean, intuitive design.
  • No registration required—just open the app and start adding workouts
  • Easily move and edit workouts to match real life
  • Visualize your weekly distance or time with clear, beautiful charts
Train your way. Focus on the work. Let the app handle the rest.

Privacy Policy

This app does not collect any personal data. All data is stored locally on your device. The app does not use any third-party services or libraries that collect personal data.
